During my internship at **Amazon Web Services Vietnam Co., Ltd.** from **April 17, 2026** to **July 30, 2026**, I had the opportunity to learn, practice, and apply the knowledge acquired at university to a real-world working environment.

I participated in the **Comprehensive Warehouse & Transportation Management System (WMS Pro)** project. This is a complex project requiring multi-platform integration across Backend (NestJS), Web Dashboard (Next.js), and Mobile App (Flutter). Through this project, I significantly improved my skills in **Full-stack development, system architecture thinking, transactional database management (PostgreSQL), and practical Logistics operation workflows**.

Regarding my work ethic, due to the nature of focusing on independent technical research and solution development, I was always proactive in searching for documentation and resolving challenging problems. However, I am also aware of my shortcomings in communication and administrative discipline, which I aim to refine and improve in the future.

### Needs Improvement

- **Discipline**: Need to practice stricter adherence to working hours and administrative regulations of the organization, building professional working habits in a team environment.
- **Problem-solving mindset**: Although possessing strong technical execution capability, I need to expand my perspective from "writing code" to "business solutions" so that built features deliver maximum operational value for end users.
- **Communication skills**: Since the internship mainly involved working independently on the project, I realized that I need to learn to connect and communicate more with colleagues. I need to improve my ability to present technical topics simply and clearly, as well as enhance my capability to handle unexpected communication situations in daily work.
