This page summarizes the detailed 12-week worklog of the internship period at **Amazon Web Services Vietnam Co., Ltd. (Workforce Bootcamp - First Cloud AI Journey)**. Over the 12-week duration, I progressed from fundamental AWS Cloud infrastructure knowledge to analyzing, designing, developing, containerizing, and deploying a production-ready application: **WMS Pro (Comprehensive Warehouse & Transportation Management System)**.

---

* **Week 4:** [Amazon S3 Data Storage, S3 Glacier, EBS Snapshots & AWS Backup Automation](1.4-week4/)

* **Week 7:** [System Monitoring & Security with CloudWatch, CloudTrail, AWS Secrets Manager & KMS](1.7-week7/)

* **Week 9:** [Business Analysis & ERD Relational Database Design for WMS Pro Logistics System](1.9-week9/)
